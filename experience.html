WORK EXPERIENCES

Humanizing at Work Research

Center for Positive Organizations Research Assistant

I investigated how people come to see their colleagues and themselves as human beings beyond their work positions and titles. I did this through coding qualitative data from interviews regarding the connection between colleagues at work after interacting with one another in a program outside of work. I was able to use my extensive training in Positive Organizational Scholarship to help with my analysis.

Women’s Participation on Wikipedia Research

Research Assistant

I assessed what motivates experts to contribute to public information goods such as Wikipedia through my knowledge of online platforms. I analyzed the underrepresentation of women, specifically economists, on Wikipedia by creating Wikipedia pages for women economists and evaluating how Wikipedia edits these pages in relation to pages for men economists.
